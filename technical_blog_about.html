About This Blog

A Work in Progress

Welcome to my technical blog - a place where I attempt to describe various programming concepts assuming no knowledge of my audience. My goal is to write in such a way that I would have understood these topics on the day when I first learned what a computer is.

It will be readily apparent to anyone with more knowledge than I have that I am still very much a beginner. Should you find yourself aware of any misunderstandings I may have of anything I describe within this blog, feel free to email me with you insights here. I'm always open to learning more and appreciate different perpectives and constructive criticism as tools for development and growth as a programmer.

I hope you find the time you spend here to be visually pleasant. My goals for the presentation of this site are to help visitors feel invited and relaxed by using soft and calming colors and to give the sense of overall simplicity and ease of navigation. Enjoy!
